Hello!

I’m Soo-Hwan Chae, a Ph.D. candidate in the Biorobotics Lab at Seoul National University, working with Prof. Kyu-Jin Cho. My research focuses on developing a mechanism design for robotic systems, especially in biologically inspired locomotive robots.
