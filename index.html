About me

Hello! I’m Soo-Hwan Chae, a Ph.D. candidate in the Biorobotics Lab at Seoul National University, working with Prof. Kyu-Jin Cho. My research focuses on developing a mechanism design for robotic systems, especially in biologically inspired locomotive robots.

You can find more details about me, explore my research, and check out my publications. You can also download my CV.

Feel free to reach out at 0331csh@gmail.com.

Research Highlights

Small-scale jumping–driving robot with quasi-direct actuation
Agile locomotion for rough terrain exploration.

I design compact mechanisms that enable both high jumping performance and efficient driving, focusing on energy-efficient transmission and robust landing behavior.

Lightweight hip exosuit for locomotion assistance
Differential tendon-driven actuation for bilateral hip support.

I work on soft wearable robots that provide effective assistance while minimizing device weight and complexity, aiming for comfortable everyday use.
