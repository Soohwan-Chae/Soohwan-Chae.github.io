About me

Hello! I’m Soo-Hwan Chae, a Ph.D. candidate in the Biorobotics Lab at Seoul National University. My research focuses on developing innovative robotic systems, particularly in bio-inspired mechanism design and mobile robots.

I have a strong background in mechanical design and prototyping, and I am interested in combining creative mechanisms with control to achieve agile and robust robot behavior.

You can find more details about me on this page, explore my research, and check out my publications. You can also download my CV.

Research Highlights

Small-scale jumping–driving robot with quasi-direct actuation
Agile locomotion for rough terrain exploration.

I design compact mechanisms that enable both high jumping performance and efficient driving, focusing on energy-efficient transmission and robust landing behavior.
