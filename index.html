About me

Hello! I’m Soo-Hwan Chae, a Ph.D. candidate in the Biorobotics Lab at Seoul National University, working with Prof. Kyu-Jin Cho. My research focuses on developing a mechanism design for robotic systems, especially in biologically inspired locomotive robots.

You can find more details about me, explore my research, and check out my publications. You can also download my CV.

Feel free to reach out at 0331csh@gmail.com.
